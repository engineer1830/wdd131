Arizona, USA

Arizona was the 48th state to join the union in 1912. Arizona is known for the 5 C's: Copper, Cattle, Cotton, Citrus, and Climate. Arizona is the top copper producing state in the USA. Ranching has been a staple of Arizona since the early days of people living in the state. Arizona became a major producer of Pima cotton in the 1910's. There is much citrus grown in Arizona (oranges, limes, lemons, and grapefruit. Arizona has over 300 days of sun each year and thus, the climate does draw many people to the state.
