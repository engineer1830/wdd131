Resources - Where to take people

Guides - be careful what you pay for. Studies show that 92% of professional money managers do not beat the S&P 500 market long-term. (https://www.fool.com/investing/2023/11/26/want-to-outperform-92-of-professional-fund-manager/) That means paying for an actively managed fund pays for itself 8% of the time. A 1% fee sounds minor, but 1% of a $1,000,000 portfolio means you're paying someone $10,000 for a service that does not outperform the market 92% of the time. In order to breakeven, they need to beat the market by at least 1.1% before value is realized.
