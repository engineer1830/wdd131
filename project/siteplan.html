Philosophy and Principles

Investing philosophy . . . understand what you invest in. Keep it simple What are the various vehicles (some). How do I prepare for retirement when it is many years away? Understand compound interest and the value of having an investing strategy for all phases. Start saving early . . . consistently. . . never touch it. When do you want to retire? Roth vs Traditional ETF vs Mutual Funds Index Funds vs Actively Managed Don't retire from something . . . retire to something. Phases can be viewed with respect to age or with respect to how long before you want to retire The phases control how aggressive or conservative you need to be with your money. When you have 30 years before retirement, a down cycle in the market is meaningless as you were not ready to touch the money anyway. When you have 30 months before your planned retirement, a down cycle in the market may affect when you do something.
