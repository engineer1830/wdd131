Purpose

Why This Website

I have lived and worked a few years in a economically changing world and I've seen boom time and bust time in the financial systems. I desire to document and share the lessons that we've learned so that my adult children have a resource to rely upon as they navigate their lives. This site is "planting trees that will produce fruit that I will never eat myself. It is for those who follow. I have tried to create simply summaries of key information with guidance based on my experience. I have also created a tool that models the potential results of a lifetime of discipline based on my recommendations of investment strategies and portfolio balancing.

Summary of Resources of this website

Philosophy — Deeper Exploration of Hamilton Principles
Phases — Breaking down investment balance through various phases by recommended age
Vehicles — High level introduction and summary of common investment vehicles
Resources — Recommended resources that will provide much more in-depth information
